About

WORDS OF MANAGEMENT

EZAR Company is today one of the actors of the engineering in Africa. The versatile structure of our office allows us to adapt flexibly to the demands of its market. The motivation and effort of all EZARS employees is the key to our performance. Given the needs of the African continent, we have given priority to South-South collaboration. Our primary concern is to respond to needs by providing them with services adapted to evolve and build sustainability. Our services are increasingly well-known thanks to the high degree of technical skill achieved by our staff and the result of continuous improvement of quality standards, enabling us to be a potential partner in engineering today and tomorrow.

HUMAN RESSOURCES

The quality and efficiency of the service rendered is at the heart of EZAR Company’s concerns. This is why the office is recruiting its engineers and technicians. Selected with great demand for their technical skills.

EZAR Company has a fairly large human potential that can develop specific capacities in relation to a expressed need.
